About me


I'm Shayan Edalatmanesh, a theoretical physicist and an Open-source enthusiast / contributor. I mainly use Python for scientific purposes 🐍

I'm currently working at Nanosurf lab in Prague, Czech Republic. I'm interested in studying the electronic structure and properties of different nanomaterials, using density functional theory and other theoretical modeling tools.

To help interpret the experimental data, I perform simulations of scanning probe microscopy (AFM, STM and KPFM) on nanostructures. There I use the Probe-Particle package, where I'm also a (co-)developer (PPSTM).
